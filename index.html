Hyperfocus

  • Hyperfocus is the ability to intentensly fixate on a task for extended periods of time without pause.
  • It is the ability to concentrate on a task to the extent that time no longer has meaning, the rest of the world melts away, and individuals in it no longer feel hunger, exhaustion, or other physiological signals.
  • People who typically hyperfocus can work for hours on end without noticing that any time has passed.
