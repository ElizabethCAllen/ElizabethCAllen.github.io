1. Hyperfocus

Hyperfocus is the ability to fixate on a task for extended periods without pause. It is the ability to concentrate on a task to the extent that time no longer has meaning. The rest of the world melts away, and individuals in it no longer feel hunger, exhaustion, or other physiological signals.
People who typically hyperfocus can work for hours on end without noticing that any time has passed.

ADA and Web Accessibility

I specialize in Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) compliance for websites.
